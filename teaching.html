Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy centers on making complex technical concepts accessible and engaging through hands-on learning and real-world applications. I believe in:

  • Active Learning: Creating interactive experiences like the "Ollie the Otter" AWS tutorial that transform abstract concepts into memorable adventures
  • Ethical Integration: Weaving discussions of ethics and bias throughout technical instruction, particularly in AI and data science contexts
  • Inclusive Education: Designing learning experiences that welcome students from diverse backgrounds and skill levels
  • Practical Application: Connecting classroom learning to real-world problems in animal welfare, technology, and social responsibility
